What is Microsoft365.com/setup?

Microsoft365.com/setup is an official portal to activate and start your Microsoft 365 product including office apps, cloud services and other collaboration services. You’ll need to Sign In and enter Microsoft 365 product key.


Microsoft 365 product key

A Microsoft 365 product key is a 25-character code that is used to activate Microsoft 365 products, including Office 365, Windows 10, and other related services. The product key is usually provided when you purchase a Microsoft 365 subscription or a one-time purchase of the software. The product key is usually a combination of letters and numbers, formatted like this: XXXXX-XXXXX-XXXXX-XXXXX-XXXXX


Create Microsoft365.com/setup Account?

  • Go to Microsoft365.com.
  • Click on "Create a Microsoft account".
  • Enter your email address (or phone number).
  • Choose a password (and confirm it).
  • Enter your first and last name.
  • Click "Next".
  • Verify your email address (or phone number) by entering the verification code sent to you.
  • Click "Verify".
  • Set up your account by adding a profile picture, setting up security questions, and reviewing the terms of service.
  • Click "Create account".

How do I set up Microsoft Office 365?

  • Go to "microsoft 365 download" and sign in with your Microsoft account.
  • Enter your product key (if you have one) or purchase a subscription.
  • Choose your plan (Personal, Home, or Business) and click "Next".
  • Download and install the Office 365 software.
  • Run the installation file and follow the prompts.
  • Accept the terms and conditions.
  • Choose whether to install the full version or a trial version.
  • Wait for the installation to complete.
  • Activate Office 365 by signing in with your Microsoft account.
  • Set up your account by adding a profile picture, setting up security questions, and reviewing the terms of service.

How to activate Microsoft 365 with product key?

  • Go to "microsoft365.com/setup login" and sign in with your Microsoft account.
  • Enter your product key (25-character code) in the "Enter product key" field.
  • Click "Next" to verify the product key.
  • Select your country or region and language.
  • Click "Next" to accept the terms and conditions.
  • Choose whether to install the full version or a trial version.
  • Wait for the activation process to complete.
  • You will see a confirmation message indicating that Microsoft 365 has been activated successfully.
